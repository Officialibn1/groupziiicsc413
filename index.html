WHAT IS CYBER LAW

Cyber law, also known as Internet law or information technology law, refers to the legal framework that governs activities in the digital realm. It encompasses a wide range of legal issues related to the use of computers, the internet, and technology. Cyber law addresses various aspects such as online privacy, data protection, intellectual property rights, electronic transactions, cybercrime, and the legal responsibilities and liabilities of individuals and organizations in cyberspace.
